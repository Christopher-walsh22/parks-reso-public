Safety information
Be prepared. Create a trip plan and get to know the AdventureSmart's 3Ts.
Check the park page for advisories.
Be Bear Aware. Avoid or stow attractants to help avoid encounters.
Pack enough water, or be prepared to treat fresh water. There are no signifigant water sources beyond the 5 km mark on the trail until you reach Elfin Lakes at 11 km.
Visitor guidelines
Biking is permitted to Cheakamus Lake and Singing Creek but not Helm Creek. Use bike-specific paths when available, and be considerate of hikers.
Respect other park users. Do not play music out loud.
There are no garbage cans along the trail. Understand your responsibility to #LeaveNoTrace by packing out anything you pack in.
Pets are not permitted in Garibaldi Park.
